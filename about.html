Mission

To empower individuals and families with the right insurance protection and financial security through trustworthy, simple, and value-based services.

We also aim to uplift society by creating income opportunities for unemployed youth, women, and retired individuals through flexible and independent work in the insurance sector.

Vision

To be a trusted, people-first insurance service provider while creating independent earning opportunities across India. We empower skilled, IRDAI-certified advisors to build their careers and help customers access safe and transparent bank auction property solutions.

About The Founder

Mr. Kamar Ali, Founder and Owner of Snow Hills Multi Services, is a passionate entrepreneur with a strong professional foundation in the automobile industry. As an Automobile Engineer, he served successfully for 19 years before choosing to follow his ambition of supporting people and creating positive change.

With a vision to empower society, he established Snow Hills as an independent organization to provide career opportunities as IRDAI-certified Insurance Advisors. His goal is to help unemployed youth build stable careers with dignity and financial growth.

He is also committed to women empowerment and actively encourages women to join the insurance sector, achieve financial independence, and lead confident lives.
